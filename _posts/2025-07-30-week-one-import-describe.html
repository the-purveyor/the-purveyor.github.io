Note that the names that appeared in the list of countries whose GDP data is â€œmissingâ€ are appearing in the list of countries whose GDP is USD 10 trillion or more. Why?

This is missing numeric data displayed by a "." (dot) is stored as a very very (unreasonably) high value by Stata. Since Stata treats . > 10000000000000, the names of those countries with "." (missing data) shows-up in the list of countries whose GDP is 10 trillion or more. The right approach is to list those countries that have GDP of USD 10 trillion or more AND do not have missing GDP data.

. * List country names whose GDP >= USD 10 trillion & GDP is non-missing.
. list CountryName if                                     ///
>     Constant_GDP2022 >= 10000000000000 &                ///
>     Constant_GDP2022 != .
